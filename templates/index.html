About
VINCENT CHIEYINE & CO.

THE FIRM’S HISTORY
Vincent Chieyine & Co. was founded in the year 1998 to provide high-quality legal services to various people at various levels of society. It took off first in Lagos State but moved its office to Enugu, Enugu State, in the year 2000. The founder and Head of Chambers, Vincent Chieyine, Esq., was called to the Nigerian Bar in 1992. He had his tutelage and was an associate in the prestigious law firm of Messrs Impey & Coker - a world-class law firm based in Lagos, Nigeria.

Since its establishment, Vincent Chieyine & Co. has grown from strength to strength, handling matters of various textures and complexities. It operates from the following major Departments: General Litigation, Mediation and Arbitration, Property and Commercial Law Practices, and Corporate Practice and Regulatory Compliance.

General Litigation: The firm handles disputes at all levels of courts, from Customary Courts, Magistrate Courts, Customary Courts of Appeal, High Courts (both at the Federal and State levels), Court of Appeal, and the Supreme Court. Some landmark cases include:

  • MAGNA MARITIME SERVICES LTD & ANOR. VS S.A. OTEJU & ANOR. (2005) ALL FWLR (PT.270)1995; (2005) 6 CLRN 43 S.C.
  • SOESON LTD VS. AFRIBANK (NIG) PLC (2000) 4 NWLR (PT. 633)391
  • OTEJU VS. MAGNA MARITIME SERVICES LTD (2000)1 NWLR (PT. 640)331
  • ISC SERVICES LTD VS. GENAK CONTINENTAL LTD & ANOR. (2006) 4 CLRN 59
Following the growing trend and benefits of Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR), the firm has always encouraged parties to embrace ADR and to resort to litigation where it becomes inevitable. The firm has a department devoted to Arbitration and Mediation and has contributed immensely to mediation at the Enugu State Multi-Door Courthouse. Commercial and Property Law Practice: This department is among the top in the country. It provides management services, legal documentation, land documentation and registration, and corporate services, among others. Vincent Chieyine & Co. is a law firm of choice for companies and small-scale businesses in need of corporate and regulatory compliance services, legal advisory services, and company secretarial services.
